## Tips for Getting First Jobs:

1. **Apply to jobs with < 5 proposals** — less competition
2. **Write custom proposals** — don't copy-paste
3. **Mention specific tech** from the job post
4. **Show relevant portfolio** items
5. **Price slightly lower** for first 5 jobs to build reviews
6. **Deliver early** — underpromise, overdeliver
7. **Ask for reviews** — politely, after successful delivery
